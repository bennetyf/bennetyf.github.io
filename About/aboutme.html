Biography

Feng is currently working as a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), University of New South Wales (UNSW), under the supervision of Professor Boualem Benatallah and Dr. Lina Yao and holds the Scientia PhD Scholarship. Feng's research topic lies within machine learning, data mining and cognitive computing. Prior to joining UNSW, Feng worked as a firmware engineer at Palette Pty., Ltd., Australia, responsible for developing the embedded systems of the company's main product. Feng also holds a Master of Philosophy (MPhil) degree from the University of Melbourne (UoM), majoring in electrical and electronics engineering. During the MPhil study, Feng mainly worked on innovative designs of short-to-medium reach optical communication systems. Before admitted by UoM, Feng graduated from the University of Science and Technology of China with a Bachelor of Engineering degree focusing on automatic control. During the undergraduate years, Feng had the opportunity to work as an intern in Chinese Academy of Sciences.
