Feng Yuan

PHD STUDENT,  COMPUTER SCIENCE

Feng Yuan

Feng is currently a PhD candidate in Computer Science and Engineering, University of New South Wales. Prior to joining UNSW, Feng worked in Palette, Pty., Ltd., Australia as a firmware engineer. Feng holds a Master of Philosophy degree from Electrical and Electronics Engineering, University of Melbourne, and a Bachelor of Engineering degree from the University of Science and Technology of China. Feng has rich research experience in optical communication and industry experience in embedded system development.
